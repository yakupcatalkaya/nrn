12 /*
13 Started out attempting a general implementation in which the subtrees could be
14 on any host and any number of them. That required a distinct gid for each
15 subtree. However, since the only purpose of cellsplit is for load balance
16 efficiency, we settled on a very restrictive but still adequate policy of
17 subtrees being on adjacent hosts and at most one cell split between i and i+1
18 and at most one cell split between i and i-1. We also disallow a split where
19 both subtrees are on the same host. This policy allows a very simple and direct
20 setting up and transfer of matrix information. Note that gid information about
21 the subtrees is no longer required by this implementation.
